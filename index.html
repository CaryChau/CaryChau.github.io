Future Career Plan

My long-term ambition is to create games that preserve, promote and revitalize local culture through interactive storytelling. Rather than treating games solely as entertainment, I believe games can become digital cultural artifacts that allow players to experience traditions, languages, folklore, industries and historical memories in ways that books and videos cannot. My goal is to combine software engineering, simulation systems and game design to build meaningful experiences that connect younger generations with their cultural roots while remaining engaging to international audiences.
